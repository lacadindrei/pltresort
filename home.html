PLT Group of Companies Wellness and Mountain Resort


A taste of paradise in Nueva Vizcaya


VISION: A priority destination of guests and visitors in the region as a place of relaxation, team building and family gathering.

MISSION: To provide a spectacular experience to guests and visitors as a result of quality service in a safe, happy, healthy, comfortable and friendly environment.


Enhanced by art

AIMS: To give prompt and efficient service to our guests by responding to their immediate needs and welfare.
To provide a safe and humane environment to our staff.
To provide a professional employee-employer relationship by adopting a culture of openness, teamwork, respect, commitment and dedication.


HAVE MEMORABLE MOMENTS

As the venue of big functions of government and non government organizations, a dormitory with 180 bed capacity was added as a place for the participants and guests as well to stay. This is also used by big groups of transients attending other functions in Nueva Vizcaya.


With the influx of the clients and guests, the couple decided to expand the facility by building a 4,000 sqm pool known as the GIGANTIC POOL. With its wide and huge area it was called the "OCEAN IN THE VALLEY,"


A Desired Serenity

Today, PLT WELLNESS AND MOUNTAIN RESORT becomes a priority destination of so many guests not only in the nearby provinces but in other parts of the country as well. It possesses a mystical beauty that enchants resort goers because of its beautiful scenery. A legendary story as related by the old folks in the locality and by the Chairman of the Tourism Council of the Sangunian Panlalawigan Hon. Roland Carub that "Singian Mountain where this resort is located has a magical spell that whoever wishes for something good, it is always awarded". It has been declared by the Solano Municipal Council through a local ordinance that Singian Mountain is part of the Eco Tourism development.


After five years of quality service to its clientele, PLT Wellness and Mountain Resort was rated by the Regional Tourism Council as ONE STAR HOTEL as we continue to develop its services to cater to a more wider range of guests and visitors. The vision of being a priority destination of guests and visitors for relaxation, team building and family gathering will always prevail in a safe, healthy, happy and wholesome environment.
